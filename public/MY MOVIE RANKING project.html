Good Movies According To Me


My Top 3 Of Alltimes.

A Silent Voice.

Great Animations and a good story about a Girl who is deaf and a Boy who used to Bully her. Watch how the boy Matures and regrets his Actions.

The Batman Trilogy.

The three Movies in this trilogy are full of Action ,Sci-fi and Thrill. A must Watch from me.

The Spider-man series.

A Masterpiece from the legend Stan Lee. These movies carried my whole childhood and is continuing to do so. Tobey Maguire is my favourite actor that played the spider man.
